## Create office hours

1. **Frequency**: [weekly](#weekly) or [one-off](#one-off)
2. **Day**: weekly or a specific day depending on the **frequency** setting
3. **Contact**: police station or service
4. **Closed**
5. **Appointment only**
6. **Opening time**
7. **Closing time**
8. **Additional note**
9. **Published state**
